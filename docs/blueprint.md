# **App Name**: Biblical Atlas

## Core Features:

- Interactive Map: Display biblical events on an interactive map for a visual understanding of their locations.
- Image Gallery: Showcase a gallery of high-resolution images of biblical locations, artifacts, and reconstructions.
- Module-based Content: Organize content into modules based on biblical eras, each containing relevant images and information.
- Bible Verse Integration: Display relevant Bible verses alongside corresponding locations and images.
- Geographic Insights: AI-powered tool that generates insights about biblical geography using advanced maps. Includes topographical analysis of locations and automated connections of related geographical regions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep gold (#D4AF37) to convey richness, value, and importance in line with religious themes.
- Background color: Dark charcoal (#333333) to establish a premium and trustworthy aesthetic with a sense of gravitas.
- Accent color: Soft gold (#E6C75C) to highlight calls to action and key information, ensuring they stand out against the dark backdrop.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font to ensure readability, a vintage feel, and an intellectual impression
- Use elegant, minimalist icons in gold to represent different modules and features.
- Employ a clear visual hierarchy with generous spacing to ensure a premium, trustworthy, and educational look.
- Incorporate subtle animations to enhance user engagement without being distracting, such as fading effects on image transitions and smooth scrolling.
